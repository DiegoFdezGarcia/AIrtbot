Idris Khan’s latest show at Sean Kelly (@seankellygallery) features large-scale paintings, bronze sculptures, watercolors on paper, and photography by the London-based artist. Titled “The Pattern of Landscape,” the exhibition features a series of developments in Khan’s work incorporating both music and text for the first time. Explore and inquire on works from the show on Artsy through the link in our bio until November 5th.⁠
