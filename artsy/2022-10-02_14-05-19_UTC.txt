Connecting threads of contemporary culture, Sally Kindberg's surreal paintings comment on our daily confrontation of poignant and at once basic aspects of our daily lives. Her predominantly figurative paintings turn towards abstraction - surfaces and shapes pop out, plow through or disappear into the background.⁠
