In its latest exhibition, Bernard Jacobson Gallery (@jacobsongallery) presents still lifes by Georges Braque, William Tillyer, Bruce McLean, Matthew Smith, and Tom Wesselmann, among others. Titled “Object / Subject: The Art of Still Life,” the exhibition positions the still life genre as one of constant transformation and regeneration. Explore and inquire on works from the show on Artsy through the link in our bio until August 26th.⁠
