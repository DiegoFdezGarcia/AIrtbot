Artists like Sarah Slappey, Ambera Wellmann, Madeleine Roger-Lacan, and Naudline Pierre have reintroduced through their surrealistic paintings more nuanced expressions of sex and the body. The revival of sex and Surrealism exceeds mere arousal, and functions as an opportunity for painters to more fully realize anxieties and experiences pertaining to womanhood.⁠
