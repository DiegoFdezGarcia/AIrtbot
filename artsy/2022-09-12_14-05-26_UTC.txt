Paris-based artist Lucile Gauvain presents figurative works that are rooted in fantasy. Using only colored pencils, the artist delves deep into the imagination, embracing uncanny narratives and exploring themes of truth and illusion. Inspired by cinematic stories and motion pictures, Gauvain creates fictional worlds that transcend reality. She portrays ordinary human interactions within unconventional spaces, incorporating surrealistic elements into her works.⁠
