While Seoul is lauded as the new art destination in Asia through a western perspective, the Korean capital has long been immersed in art. Kiaf Seoul, founded by the 46-year-old organization Galleries Association of Korea, has been operating within Seoul’s ecosystem since 2002. This year, however, the art fair has opened its most international edition to date with the company of Frieze, proving that joining forces in a competitive market can indeed yield a win-win. Artsy roamed Kiaf Seoul’s booths to share six of the most exciting artists of Korean descent to know.⁠
