The latest group show at OMR (@galeriaomr) features a diverse presentation of Mexican and international artists from the gallery’s program. Titled “Salón OMR,” the exhibition features work by Pia Camil, Gabriel Rico, Troika, Atelier Van Lieshout, Jose Dávila, Christian Jankowsky, Sebastián Silva, and Eduardo Sarabia. Collect works from the show on Artsy through the link in our bio until October 28th.⁠
