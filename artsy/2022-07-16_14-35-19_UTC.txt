The Haas Brothers—twins Simon and Nikolai—are known for designing charming, playful furniture and objects inspired by nature, science fiction, and psychedelia. Cleverly employing a variety of materials—such as brass, porcelain, plexiglass, resin, and fur—they create bulbous, textured alien forms that seem plucked from a children’s story: The Haas Brothers’ sleek forms nevertheless retain traces of artisanal handiwork.⁠
