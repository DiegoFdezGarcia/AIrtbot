Sometimes cheerful, sometimes worried, the work of Hervé Priou celebrates the movements of our transient era. Beginning with the idea that reality is as flexible as plural, the artist uses those changes as a register of shapes which he is using to create, step by step, a singular picture of reality. ⁠
