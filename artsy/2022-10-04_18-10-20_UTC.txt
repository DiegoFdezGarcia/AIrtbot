Brook Hsu’s latest show at Kiang Malingue (@kiangmalingue) showcases more than 30 paintings and drawings made throughout 2021 and 2022. Titled “Oranges, Clementines, and Tangerines,” the exhibition explores a host of the artist’s favorite themes and motifs, demonstrating her continued negotiation with the history of painting and with contemporary existential issues. Explore and inquire on works from the show on Artsy through the link in our bio until November 12th.⁠
