The latest show at Villepin (@villepin_art) features masterpieces by Francis Bacon in dialogue with contemporary artists Adrian Ghenie, Zeng Fanzhi, George Condo, and Yukimasa Ida. Titled “The Loss of Human Face?,” the exhibition expresses how artists have used the face to reflect human truths. Explore and inquire on works from the show on Artsy through the link in our bio until November 30th.⁠
