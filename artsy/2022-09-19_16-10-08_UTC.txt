"In Residence" at Mexico City's JO-HS Gallery (@_jo_hs) brings together new works by five artists who participated in the gallery's 2022 summer residency program. The artists—Gabriela Maskrey, Jack Mernin, Antoine Lussier, Pedro Lavin, and Rose Barberat—have drawn inspiration from their experiences in Mexico's vibrant capital.⁠ Explore the viewing room through the link in our bio until October 31st.⁠
