Breakfast at Tiffany & Co. to unveil “I Shine, You Shine, We Shine,⁠” a new artwork by Derrick Adams that represents a never-ending pursuit of love and compassion. Commissioned by Tiffany & Co. to celebrate the launch of Tiffany Atrium, the brand’s new social impact platform, the work is available for bidding on Artsy in support of The Last Resort Artist Retreat, an artist residency program founded by Adams in his hometown of Baltimore, Maryland.
