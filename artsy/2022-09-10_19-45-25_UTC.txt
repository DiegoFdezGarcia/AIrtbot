The latest show of works by Anna-Eva Bergman at Perrotin (@galerieperrotin) marks the first survey exhibition in the United States of the late Norwegian-born painter. Titled “Revelation,” the exhibition tracks the journey of a singular woman through the trials and tribulations of the 20th century. Explore and inquire on works from the show on Artsy through the link in our bio until October 22nd.⁠
