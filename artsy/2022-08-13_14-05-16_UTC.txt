American painter and sculptor Anne Vieux intentionally misuses tools like scanners and holographic paper to create psychedelic digital paintings with a technological Moiré effect. Her glowing abstract compositions mimic a warped computer screen as a substitute for the way in which a window was traditionally understood in Western art as a window onto another world.⁠
